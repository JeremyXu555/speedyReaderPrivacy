Privacy Policy

Last updated on December, 2023

Thank you for using Speedy Reader Chrome Extension! This privacy policy outlines how the extension handles user data and ensures transparency regarding privacy practices.

Information We Collect

The SpeedyReader Chrome Extension may collect the contents include personal identifiable information that are already publicly accessible on the website the users are browsing, and send to HuggingFace for summary. But SpeedyReader Chrome Extension does not collect any personal identifiable information from its users on purpose. The extension is designed to respect user privacy, and we do not track, store, or share any personal data.

Permissions

    Speedy Reader Chrome Extension requests the following permissions for specific functionalities:
  • tabs: Required to interact with browser tabs for the extension's functionality.
  • storage: Necessary for storing extension-specific data on your device.

Content Scripts

The extension may inject content scripts into web pages for improved functionality. These scripts are designed to enhance the browsing experience and do not collect any user data.

Data Storage

Any data stored by Speedy Reader Chrome Extension is limited to local storage and is used solely for the purpose of providing the intended features of the extension.

Third-Party Services

The contents will be sent to a 3rd party AI provider called HuggingFace for summary. HuggingFace is a trusted and leading AI company built by the AI community that aims to democratize AI. See here for the HuggingFace privacy policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically for any changes.
